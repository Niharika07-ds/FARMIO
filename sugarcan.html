Sugarcane

Sugarcane is a tall perennial grass belonging to the genus Saccharum. It is widely cultivated for its high sugar content and is one of the main sources of sugar production across the world, especially in tropical regions.

Why Sugarcane Is Grown in This Season

Sugarcane requires warm temperatures, abundant sunlight, and sufficient water. Tropical and subtropical climates provide ideal growing conditions, supporting healthy stalk development and high sugar accumulation.

Benefits of Growing Sugarcane

Sugarcane has high commercial value and multiple uses such as sugar, jaggery, ethanol, molasses, and biofuel production. It provides good income and employment opportunities for farmers.

Sugarcane Farming Tips

  • Use healthy and disease-free setts for planting.
  • Grow in deep, fertile, well-drained soil.
  • Ensure regular irrigation, especially in early stages.
  • Apply organic manure and fertilizers as needed.
  • Control pests and diseases through proper management.
