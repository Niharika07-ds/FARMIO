Cotton

Cotton (Gossypium) is a major commercial fiber crop grown in tropical and subtropical regions. It is the primary raw material for the textile industry and plays an important role in the agricultural economy of India.

Why Cotton Is Grown in This Season

Cotton is a Kharif crop that grows best in warm climates with moderate rainfall. High temperature and sufficient sunlight help in proper boll formation and high-quality fiber production.

Uses of Cotton

  • Used to make clothes, bed sheets, and towels.
  • Medical cotton for bandages and dressings.
  • Cottonseed oil used for cooking and soap making.

Benefits of Growing Cotton

Cotton provides high income to farmers, supports textile industries, and creates employment opportunities in rural areas.
