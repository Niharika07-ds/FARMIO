Maize

Maize, also known as corn, is a major cereal crop grown worldwide. It is a staple food for humans and animals and is used in many food products and industries. Maize is rich in carbohydrates and provides high energy.

Why Maize Is Grown in This Season

Maize grows best in warm and moderately wet climates. It requires temperatures between 18°C and 27°C for proper growth. Adequate rainfall during flowering and grain formation helps in achieving high yield.

Benefits of Growing Maize

Maize has high market demand and multiple uses such as food, fodder, and industrial products. It gives good yield in a short time and provides stable income to farmers.

Maize Farming Tips

  • Use high-quality hybrid or certified seeds.
  • Grow in fertile, well-drained soil.
  • Ensure timely irrigation during critical growth stages.
  • Apply fertilizers as recommended.
  • Control weeds and pests regularly.
  • Harvest at proper maturity for best yield.
