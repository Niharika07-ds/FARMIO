Soybean

Soybean is an important oilseed and legume crop widely grown in India and many other countries. It is rich in protein and oil and plays a major role in food, animal feed, and industrial products.

Why Soybean Is Grown in This Season

Soybean grows best in warm and moist climatic conditions. It requires temperatures between 20°C and 30°C along with moderate rainfall. Kharif season provides ideal conditions for good growth and yield.

Benefits of Growing Soybean

Soybean improves soil fertility by fixing nitrogen. It has high market demand for oil extraction and protein-rich food products, providing good income to farmers.

Soybean Farming Tips

  • Use certified and disease-resistant seed varieties.
  • Grow in well-drained loamy soil.
  • Sow seeds at proper spacing.
  • Provide moderate irrigation; avoid waterlogging.
  • Apply fertilizers as per recommendations.
  • Control weeds and pests regularly.
