PM-Kisan Samman Nidhi

Launched in 2019, the PM-Kisan Samman Nidhi scheme provides ₹6,000 per year to eligible farmers in three installments of ₹2,000 each directly into their bank accounts.

📌 Latest Updates (2026)

  • The 21st installment of ₹2,000 was released on 19 November 2025.
  • The 22nd installment of ₹2,000 is expected between February–March 2026 (official date pending).
  • Farmers must complete eKYC and Aadhaar-bank linking to receive payments without delay.
  • A unique Farmer ID is now required for future installments.

✔️ Eligibility

Small and marginal farmers with landholding records who are not high-income taxpayers can avail the benefits. Documents must be kept updated to remain eligible.

🌾 Benefits

The scheme provides direct income support for agricultural expenses like seeds, fertilizers, and other inputs, ensuring transparency and reducing middlemen.
