Rice

Rice is one of the most important staple food crops in the world, especially in Asian countries. It belongs to the grass family and is mainly grown in water-rich fields called paddy fields. Rice provides a major source of energy for millions of people.

Why Rice Is Grown in This Season

Rice grows best in warm and humid climates with plenty of water. It requires temperatures between 20°C and 35°C and high rainfall. Monsoon season provides ideal conditions for rice cultivation.

Benefits of Growing Rice

Rice has high demand and ensures food security. It provides employment to a large population and supports rural economies. Rice straw is also used as animal feed and fuel.

Rice Farming Tips

  • Use high-yielding and disease-resistant varieties.
  • Prepare well-leveled fields for water retention.
  • Maintain standing water during early growth stages.
  • Apply fertilizers at recommended stages.
  • Control weeds and pests regularly.
  • Harvest when grains are fully mature.
