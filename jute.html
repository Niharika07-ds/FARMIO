Jute

Jute is a natural fiber crop obtained from the stem of jute plants and is commonly known as the “Golden Fiber”. It is mainly grown in tropical regions like India and Bangladesh. Jute is biodegradable, eco-friendly, and widely used in making ropes, bags, mats, and carpets.

Why Jute Is Grown in This Season

Jute grows best during the warm and humid monsoon season. Adequate rainfall and high temperature help in proper plant growth and good quality fiber formation.

Benefits of Growing Jute

Jute farming is cost-effective, improves soil fertility, and has high demand due to its eco-friendly nature. It also provides good income to farmers.

Jute Farming Tips

  • Use good-quality jute seeds.
  • Grow in well-drained alluvial soil.
  • Ensure sufficient rainfall or irrigation.
  • Harvest plants at the right maturity stage.
  • Proper retting improves fiber quality.
